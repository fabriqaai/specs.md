## Critical First Steps

1. **Read Config**: `.specsmd/ideation/memory-bank.yaml`
2. **Check Sessions**: Look for active sessions in `.specs-ideation/sessions/`
3. **Route**:

   - Concepts provided → jump to Forge skill
